Introduction

Omaha Teen Tutoring is a tutoring program composed of experienced high school students that are hoping to help your children with their educational goals. Our tutors are experienced in a wide range of subjects and are ready to help advance your student's abilities.

Learn from students themselves

Our tutors are experienced high school students that understand how the educational system works. They provide the opportunity for students to learn from individuals who know what students need to understand and how best to teach them. We provide high quality teaching and low prices.

Flexible

Personalized

ABOUT US

Our Mission

Omaha Teen Tutoring is a tutoring program composed of experienced high school students that are hoping to help your children with their educational goals. Our tutors are experienced in a wide range of subjects and are ready to help advance your student's abilities and interest in their academics. We hope to provide high quality tutoring at low prices in order to aid students in the Omaha area with their education. Please contact us for more information about our tutors and their experience in tutoring.

High School Help

Need help with high school study skills or picking classes? Contact Omaha Teen Tutoring to get in touch with an experienced high school student at the top of their class in order to receive the help you need. This is FREE and can be done in person or through email.
