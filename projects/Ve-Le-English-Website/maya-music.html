Music

Before Angelou's famed future as an author and poet, she was a singer and dancer - specifically, to calypso music. She wrote multiple screenplays and sang and starred in them! She recorded her own album, Miss Calypso, where her deep voice captivated the audience. She learned modern and African style dances and taught it to others. She would also professionally dance for others.
