EXPLORING CREATIVE DEPTHS

Writing 7 autobiographies, 3 books of essays, and 5 books of poetry, Maya Angelou has only served to inspire students and teachers alike. Her ability to provide a new perspective in a relatable and comfortable manner only serves to emphasize her lessons even more.

A LASTING JOURNEY

Born on April 4, 1928, Maya Angelou was destined to educate and encourage others. Having won over 30 awards and honors, Angelou has made a lasting impact on not only the African-American community, but generations to come.
